Ex - Typical Convolution Problem

$a_{1\cdots n} \in [1,2\cdot 10^5]$

$f_0 = 1$
$f_k = a_k \cdot (\sum_{i+j<k} f_i\cdot f_j)$

求所有 $f_{1\cdots n}\mod 998244353$

5s

1024mb

我的思路

令 $G_k = \sum_{i+j=k} F_iF_j$

$G = F\star F$

$F_k=A_k(\sum_{i<k} G_i)$
